Pittsburgh is a city in the Commonweatlth of Pennsylvania and the seat of Allegheny County. It is the most popular city in both Allegheny County and Western Pennsylvania, the second-most populous city in Pennsylvania after Phily. Pittsburgh is located in the southwest Pennsylvania at the confluence of the Allegheny River and the Monongahela River, which Combine to form the Ohio River. Pittsburgh is know both as "The Steel City" for its dominnant role in the history of the U.S. steel industy, and as the "City of Bridges for its 446 bridges.
